THE PLAYBOOK

Game Rules

Mastering GridRush requires speed and absolute logic. Review the framework below to secure your victory:

01

Roll & Setup

Roll the coordinate dice to place your blockades on the shared 6x6 grid mapping system.

02

Solve the Grid

Fill out your grid segments concurrently. Every correct solution unlocks movement points.

03

Beat the Clock

You have exactly 15 seconds per round. Fail to match the grid layout, and your token stays static.
